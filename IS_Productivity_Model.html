Brightwork Time Tracking Productivity and Project Pipeline Capacity Model¶

How do we utilize our time and what is our future projects load?¶

Assumption:¶

Accuracy of analysis is highly dependent on data entered in BrightWork. Time frame of analyzed data is from Jul 1 2017 until last 2 weeks of current date. This time frame provides optimal data quality and volume.
